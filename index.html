Smart Energy Efficiency Solutions

How Technology Can Help Fight Climate Change in Buildings and Cities

As climate change accelerates, energy consumption in cities and buildings has become a major contributor to global emissions. However, emerging technologies offer smart, data-driven solutions to improve energy efficiency, reduce waste, and build a sustainable future.

This project explores how informatics—through IoT, artificial intelligence, and digital innovation—can transform our approach to energy, making our urban spaces smarter and greener.
